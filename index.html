The Game

You are the lone IT engineer of a behemoth colony ship headed for the far reaches of the galaxy. Unfortunately for you and the colony, your ship’s computer has caught a nasty virus, and it’s wreaking havoc with your systems. You can only wake from hyper-sleep for so long before you run out of oxygen, so you better use your time and resources wisely.
